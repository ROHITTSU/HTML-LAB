Lists in HTML.....

  • Unordered list( UL tag ):
    1. These lists are used for items that do not need to be in any
      specific order . The list items are typically marked with bullet points.
  • Ordered list( OL tag ):
    1. These lists are used when the order of the items is important
      Each item in an ordered list is typically marked with number or letter

This is the Example of Unordered List.

  • Rohit
  • Rahul
  • ALinavaz

This is the Example of Ordered List.

  1. Rohit
  2. Rahul
  3. ALinavaz
